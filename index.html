About

SmashWise Interactive is a brand new interactive agency with roots in Toronto, Ontario. Originally slated to take off in January 2016, a lacklustre website combined with a weak portfolio made for a less than convincing argument as to why SmashWise should make your next website or app. After taking time to give a massive facelift to the previous website, as well as working hard to acquire new skills while building a portfolio I hope to make not choosing SmashWise a harder choice.
